About

SB Skate Collective

SB Skate Collective is a clothing and board project out of Santa Barbara, California. We make stuff we want to wear and ride — clean cuts, weird graphics, no fuss.

The mugshot dachshund is our mascot. He didn't do it. He just looks like he did.

This is a lookbook. No checkout. No waitlist.
